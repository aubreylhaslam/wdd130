About Us

Down the River Rafting is committed to helping you create memories and bond with those you love through adventure! From gentle river rides to ravenous rapids, Down the River Rafting has unforgettable experiences for all ages!

History

Down the River Rafting began it's journey with a group of ambitious college friends. Seeking the thrill of an adventure, those young college kids took a leap of faith towards their dream and began building a small white water rafting business.

Fast forward 5 years later, because of our incredible customers and your support, our dream became a reality. Our thriving business has brought opportunities and adventure to families and friends all over the world.
